Developing Intelligent Content

Class Title

ENGL 4814

Class Description

Developing Intelligent Content is an elective class I'm taking for my professional technical writing degree. In this course, we learn how to create content that may be adjusted, reused, and interacted with on multiple platforms. We do this my exploring the roles metadata, structured authoring, content management systems, and A.I. play in the process of content creation.

Professor

Dr. Carlos Evia

About the Professor

Dr. Carlos Evia is the Associate Dean of Transdisciplinary Initiatives, Chief Technology Officer, and a professor of Communications from Mexico. He earned his LCC from the Instituto de Ciencias Sociales de Merida, his MSC from the Universidad LaSalle, and his PhD from Texas Tech University. His principal research areas include technical and professional communication, user assistance, structured authoring, content strategy, and workplace safety communication.

My Review

Like many of my classes this semester, I wasn't sure what to expect going into Developing Intelligent Content. But after just a few weeks, I can definitively say it's my favorite class this semester. While I entered the class with a little experience since I write articles for a few on-campus magazines, I feel like my knowledge and ability in the subject has already grown considerably. Although a lot of the things we discuss feel intimidating at first---specifically creating websites and working in HTML---the assignments are so fun and creative that I find myself feeling excited to work through my struggles so I can achieve the final product.
